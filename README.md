# 🎵 Deep Tracks

### Follow the Rabbit Hole. Discover What Moves You.

Deep Tracks is a modern music discovery engine designed for people who are tired of hearing the same artists over and over again.

Instead of endlessly scrolling playlists, Deep Tracks helps you dive deeper into the music universe by uncovering hidden gems, exploring artist connections, discovering new genres, and learning the stories behind the artists you love.

Whether you're searching for your next favorite band, exploring a niche subgenre, or going down a musical rabbit hole at 2 AM, Deep Tracks helps you discover music that algorithms often miss.

---

## ✨ Why Deep Tracks?

Most music apps focus on playing music.

Deep Tracks focuses on **discovering music.**

This application was built for music fans who want to:

🎧 Find new artists

🎸 Discover hidden gems

🎵 Explore top tracks

📚 Learn artist history and biographies

🖼️ View artist and album artwork

🔍 Dive into genres and subgenres

🕳️ Follow connections between similar artists

🌎 Expand musical horizons beyond the mainstream
